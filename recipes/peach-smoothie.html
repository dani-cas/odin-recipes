Peach Smoothie

Peach smoothie makes for a delicious and nutritious breakfast smoothie as its blended with fresh peaches, yogurt, chia seeds and a few spices. With just a few ingredients and a high-powered blender, youâ€™ll have a healthy breakfast smoothie in minutes.

Ingredients

  • 3 peaches, pitted and quartered
  • 1/2 cup milk, diary or dairy-free
  • 1 1/2 cup yogurt, diary or dairy-free
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract
  • sprinkle of cinnamon
  • sprinkle of ground ginger
  • 2 tbsp chia seeds, divided

Optional

  • honey or maple syrup to sweeten
  • collagen powder

Steps

  1. Add all ingredients except the chia seeds to a high-powered blender. Blend on high until smooth and creamy.
  2. Divide the peach smoothie between two Le Parfait jars or Mason jars. Add one tablespoon of chia seeds, add the lid and shake until well combined.
